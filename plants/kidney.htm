Dichondra repens
Family: CONVOLVULACEAE
Dichondra repens or Kidney Weed
Perennial herb with creeping stems, rooting at nodes. Native.
Di meaning 2; Greek chondros meaning a grain, referring to the typical 2 fruitlets of the genus.
repens - Latin for creeping
The leaf is sometimes confused with that of the violet.
