Echium plantagineum
Family: BORAGINACEAE
Echium plantagineum - Paterson's Curse, Salvation Jane.
Annual, rarely biennial herb: flowers blue to purple, rarely pink to white.
Stem leaves with cordate (heart-shaped) base. Distinguish from E. vulgare whose stem leaves do not have cordate base.
From Europe.
