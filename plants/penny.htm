Hydrocotyle laxiflora
Family: APIACEAE
Hydrocotyle laxiflora or Stinking Pennywort
Perennial herb with creeping rhizomes, rooting at nodes.
Greek hydro meaning water and cotyle meaning cup or plate (referring to the little hollow in the middle of the leaf).
Latin laxa meaning loose or open, flora meaning flower.
