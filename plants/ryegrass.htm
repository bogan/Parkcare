Lolium species
Family: POACEAE
Lolium species or Ryegrass
Species identification is difficult because they hybridise readily.
Tufted annual. Exotic.
Note that this is a very different grass from Secale, Cereal Rye.
Lolium comes from the Latin for darnel - and what is darnel? A noxious weed of the genus Lolium.
