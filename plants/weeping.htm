Microlaena stipoides
Family: POACEAE
Microlaena stipoides (also referred to as Ehrharta stipoides) or Weeping Grass
Slender, tufted perennial. Native.
Palatable to stock, persistent under grazing, in good conditions remains green throughout the year.
From the Greek micros meaning small; laina meaning cloak, referring to the two outer glumes.
